ABOUT US

Our Vision

We are an emerging team of environment and life-form enthusiasts, who are working towards ensuring the safety and preservation of wildlife in our country. We wish to see humans co-existing with the fauna in the ecosystem in a harmonius manner. Our vision is to establish that coexistence in the near future

Our Mision

Most of the gap existing between the wild animals and humans are due to fear of each other. We aim to reduce that gap so that instead of running away and, creating chaos people can stay calm and get the help they deserve

Future Updates

We have plans to expand our scopes so that we can extend our services for all animals, irrespective of domestic or wild. We will also try to enhance our services so that users can get the best results from our side
